# Design Context: Sareng Digital

## Users & Purpose
- **Target Audience**: Style-conscious, digitally curious consumers in Bangladesh. Specifically urban youth, students, working professionals, and gift buyers in cities like Dhaka.
- **Job to be Done**: Finding tech accessories that serve as personality statements and conversation starters. Moving beyond "specs" to "storytelling" through desk setups and everyday carry.
- **Emotional Goals**: Confident, Delighted, Curious, Connected (to a curated aesthetic).

## Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone**: Confident but approachable, stylish but rooted, modern, and high-end archival.
- **Traits**: Bold, Curated, Playful with Purpose, Trustworthy.
- **Core Identity**: An "Obsidian Archive" or "Digital Registry" where tech accessories are treated as curated artifacts rather than mass-market inventory.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: **Neo-Brutalist Mid-Century Archival**. High impact, thick borders, raw geometric forms, and a tactile, mechanical feel.
- **Key Palette**: 
  - **Primary**: Deepest Black & Obsidian.
  - **Accent**: Sareng Gold (`#C5A059`) for "Artifact" hits.
  - **Surface**: Custom Ivory (`bg-sd-ivory`) for a premium paper/gallery feel.
  - **Highlight**: Subtle noise textures and frosted glass (Obsidian/Bone system).
- **Typography**: High-contrast, bold display faces (Neo) paired with precise, functional sub-headers. Heavy use of uppercase and tracking for archival labeling.
- **Anti-references**: Boring catalogue stores, generic "white-label" e-commerce templates, clinical specifications charts, and overly soft/rounded "tech-startup" aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. **The Archival Lens**: Every product is a "Specimen" or "Artifact." Use terminology like "Registry," "Protocol," and "Index" to elevate the shopping experience.
2. **Mechanical Tangibility**: Interactions should feel physical—staggered reveals, bold hover shifts, and deliberate transitions that mimic mechanical state changes.
3. **Intentional Contrast**: Pair raw, brutalist structural elements (thick borders, hard shadows) with refined, editorial typography and high-end product photography.
4. **Playful Precision**: Add "delight" through micro-interactions (like the dynamic favicon or live observer counts) that feel like part of a sophisticated system, not just random fluff.
5. **Where did you get that?**: Every visual choice must serve the goal of making the brand feel unique, premium, and inherently "shareable."
